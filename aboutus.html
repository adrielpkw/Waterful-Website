We are WATERFUL!

We were founded since 2020 and we are based in Malaysia.

We help connect non-profit organisations and donors around the world.








Our mission:

1. To help link users to non-profit organisations and donors around the world that helps countries in need where there are some countries facing water issues like the community is facing water cuts, lack of access to clean water and many other problems.


2. To share latest news about our precious water source from around the world and also share some tips and facts about water in order to educate people that can create awareness as well.








Our vision:

By 2030, we see that we would become one of the world's most informative website that has created a lot of awareness and educated many people on our precious water source.
